What is Streamlivr?

STREAMLIVR is a web3 livestreaming and event ticketing platform using video NFTs to connect creators and fans in the decentralized Web.

With streamlivr, every fan and supporter can attend live events, buy NFTs of their favorite Artist, Gamer, Tutor, Influence, Dnacer,COmedian and even Broadcaster. The opportunities Streamlivr offers to creators and fans are limitless.
